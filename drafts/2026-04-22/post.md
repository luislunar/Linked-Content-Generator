Every federal RFP is written in acronym.

Miss one definition → you bid wrong → you lose.

The 20 that actually decide outcomes:

GSA, MAS, SIN, UEI, SAM, NAICS, PSC, CAGE, RFP, RFQ, RFI, SOW, PWS, BPA, IDIQ, IFF, MFC, CSP-1, TAA, CPARS.

Most small businesses learn the top 5. Miss #14 (Most Favored Customer) and #18 (Contractor Performance Assessment) — and either lose the contract or the next three re-competes.

Two that cost the most when misunderstood:

→ MFC: your lowest commercial price, disclosed to GSA. Wrong disclosure = contract termination.
→ CPARS: your federal report card. One "Marginal" rating kills your next award.
