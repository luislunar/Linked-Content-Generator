# Hypothesis — 2026-07-14

This draft tests the `truth` hook pattern on the `certifications` topic using a `tips_list` format.

The opening line challenges a comfortable assumption — that earning a cert is the hard part. Most small business owners know certifications exist, but few realize the operational traps embedded in each one: the 8(a) graduation clock, the HUBZone residency rule, the SDVOSB dual-process gap. Each of the five items is a concrete, non-obvious fact that a cert holder might not discover until it costs them. That specificity should drive reactions from people who've hit these walls, and comments from business owners sharing which certification tripped them up or which they're actively pursuing. The community question is an experience-share variant designed to surface real pipelines and trigger peer-to-peer conversation in the comments.
