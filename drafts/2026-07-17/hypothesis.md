This draft tests whether a concrete data-led stat post — anchored to a real USAspending.gov figure — drives stronger reactions and comments than topical advice posts. The $11.8B Q2 2026 small-business federal awards figure makes the opportunity tangible for ICP owners who privately wonder if the federal market is "really for companies like mine." The question hook opener ("How much federal money went to small businesses last quarter?") is being tested after three consecutive statement-style openers (contrarian → blunt → truth) to see if a curiosity-gap question improves early engagement and scroll completion. The NAICS-code CTA is deliberately specific — it asks people to reveal their industry, which tends to generate more comments than generic "share your experience" closers. Expected lift: reactions from validation ("my industry is on that list"), comments from people naming their NAICS category or sharing surprise at the number's size.
