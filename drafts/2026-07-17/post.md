How much federal money went to small businesses last quarter?

In Q2 2026, the top 8 industries alone sent $11.8 billion to small business recipients — computer services, construction, engineering, and consulting leading the way. The Department of Defense awarded $5.5B of it. The VA added $3.6B. DHS another $3.2B.

Most small business owners in those exact industries have no idea that money existed. Not because the competition was too stiff — because they weren't registered to compete for it.

The federal market isn't closed to small businesses. It was designed to include them. The barrier isn't demand. It's access — the registration, the compliance, the vehicle you need to get in the door.

If you're running a business with real commercial revenue and you've been sitting on the edge of this market, these numbers are your answer.
