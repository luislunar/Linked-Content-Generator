This draft tests a `truth` hook on the `certifications` topic using a `tips_list` format with a numbered checklist structure.

The core bet: small business owners who have certifications (or are pursuing them) feel a visceral recognition when told "getting certified isn't the finish line." The hook surfaces an uncomfortable reality most content glosses over — that cert activation requires 5 paired steps most people skip. The `truth` pattern ("nobody warns you") is designed to create a "finally someone said it" moment that drives reactions from certified business owners who stalled out, and comments from people sharing where they got stuck in the activation chain. The numbered format (not `→` bullets) reinforces the checklist feel and adds visual variety after two straight prose posts. The dark-navy `single-06-checklist` template breaks the color run of two cream-bg posts. Expected movers: **reactions** from recognition among cert holders, **comments** from the community question which asks for a specific cert + a specific next step — concrete enough to prompt real answers rather than vague engagement.
