This draft tests the `alert` hook pattern applied to a `client_story` format on the `sam` topic. The choice is deliberate: SAM.gov expiration is an invisible risk that affects nearly every active federal vendor, but most small businesses don't discover it until a CO call forces the issue. Framing it as a near-miss story (rather than a tips list or warning post) grounds the compliance lesson in stakes the ICP recognizes — a real recompete, real past performance, real relationship on the line. The three-paragraph prose structure keeps it readable without bullets diluting the narrative tension. Expected impact: **reactions** should move because the fear of losing a hard-won contract is visceral and relatable; **comments** should move because the closing question ("what's your recovery plan?") surfaces a gap most people haven't thought through, prompting honest self-disclosure over shallow agreement.
