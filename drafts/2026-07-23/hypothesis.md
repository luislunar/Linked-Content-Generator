This draft tests the `contrarian` hook pattern on the `teaming` topic using a `hot_take` format. The opening moves straight into dismantling a belief the ICP holds daily — "we need past performance first" — which creates immediate tension for small business owners stuck at that mental block. The contrarian structure (wrong premise → correct reframe → proof point → blunt closing) is designed to generate reactions from readers who either recognize the mistake in themselves or want to share it with someone in their network.

Expected score impact: high reactions from the "that was me" recognition moment (business owners who've been paralyzed by the past-performance myth), and strong comments from people sharing their own teaming experience or asking follow-up questions about how to find a prime partner. The community question ("what stopped you from reaching out?") is specific enough to invite real answers rather than generic engagement.
