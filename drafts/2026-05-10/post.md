Your SAM.gov registration expired while you weren't paying attention. Happens more than you think.

Here's what a lapsed registration actually costs you:

→ You can't receive payment from any federal agency until the registration is active again — even on contracts already in place.
→ You're ineligible to bid on any new federal opportunity the moment it lapses.
→ Reactivation isn't instant — validation delays can take days to weeks.
→ Contracting officers see a non-compliant entity and flag it. That damages relationships mid-award.
→ There is no automatic renewal. SAM.gov requires you to log in and initiate it every 12 months.

The fix is easy. Log in today, find your expiration date, and set a calendar reminder 60 days out.

One check right now beats weeks of scrambling when an opportunity hits your desk.
