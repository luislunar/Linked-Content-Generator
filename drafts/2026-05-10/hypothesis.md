This draft tests an `alert` hook on a SAM.gov expiration topic using a `bullets` format. The alert pattern hasn't appeared in the last three posts (truth → myth-bust → contrarian), so it's fresh in the rotation. SAM.gov lapses are a near-universal pain point for small businesses — almost every GovCon company has either experienced a lapse or fears one, which means the hook ("expired while you weren't paying attention") should land with immediate recognition rather than needing explanation. The five-bullet structure maps one concrete consequence per bullet, keeping each item scannable and unambiguous. The community question is a soft self-disclosure ("Be honest. 😅") modeled on the highest-comment CTA style in generator.md — it invites readers to admit a real experience rather than just agree with the post. Expected impact: above-average reactions from relatability, and above-average comments driven by the honest-check prompt and the universality of the mistake.
