Before you apply for a GSA Schedule, when did you last log into SAM.gov?

I ask some version of that on almost every discovery call. It sounds like a small thing.

More often than I'd expect, the answer is: "We're registered... I think."

So we go look together. Here's what comes up more than people realize:

→ Registration expired — SAM auto-expires every 12 months, no reminder sent
→ Wrong NAICS codes — missing the categories agencies actually buy from you
→ POC info outdated — the person who registered left years ago
→ Entity name mismatch — doesn't match legal docs, triggers verification delays
→ Exclusions check skipped — rare, but it surfaces the moment you submit

None of these are hard to fix. Every one can stall a GSA Schedule application before it ever gets reviewed.

SAM.gov is the foundation. Everything else you build in federal contracting sits on top of it.

When did you last audit your SAM registration? What did you find when you looked?
