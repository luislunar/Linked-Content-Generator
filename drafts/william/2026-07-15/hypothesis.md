This draft tests whether a practical SAM audit checklist — opened with a question William actually asks on discovery calls — drives engagement through self-recognition. The hook ("when did you last log into SAM.gov?") targets a gap many small business owners don't know they have, triggering both reactions (readers who see themselves in the list) and comments (owners sharing what they found when they went to check). Expected score components to move: reactions (+) from the recognition effect of the five-error list, and comments (+) from the community question, which invites a specific, low-friction response ("I found...") rather than a broad opinion prompt.
