Before you cross federal contracts off your list because you don't have past performance — hear me out.

Almost every first call I take, I hear some version of this: "We'd love to work with the government, but we don't have any federal history yet, so..."

And that sentence trails off like the door is already closed.

It's not.

Teaming agreements exist for this exact moment. You find a prime with the contract history. They need your capabilities. You bring the work. They carry the past performance.

That's how a lot of small businesses land their first federal award.

Past performance is a real barrier — but it's not a dead end. Teaming is often the clearest path around it.
