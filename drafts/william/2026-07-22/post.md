A question I love asking at the end of a first call:

"What almost stopped you from reaching out?"

Almost every answer is one of these four:

→ **"I don't have any federal contracts yet."**
You don't need them to qualify. Two years in business and solid commercial revenue is what GSA looks for.

→ **"GSA is only for IT companies."**
The MAS covers construction, consulting, professional services, health, logistics, and more. Your industry almost certainly has a Schedule category.

→ **"The process takes years."**
It takes 6–12 months. Not years. And our team handles the 100+ hours of paperwork — you provide about three.

→ **"Once I get it, contracts will come to me."**
The Schedule opens the door. Your pipeline and follow-through walk through it.

All four beliefs are understandable. All four are costing businesses time they could spend building real federal pipeline.
