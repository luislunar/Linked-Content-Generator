This draft tests the story hook pattern on the certifications topic using prose format. The narrative arc — client frustration with WOSB inactivity, the diagnosis (missing vehicle, not broken cert), and the resolution (first award after Schedule approval) — is designed to surface two high-comment moments: readers who have lived the same frustration ("I thought my certification wasn't working either") and readers who want to share which certification moved the needle once they had a vehicle. The central insight, "multipliers of zero," is deliberately quotable and reframe-worthy, which should drive shares and saves alongside comments. Expected to move reactions (identification with the frustration) and comments (experience-shares about specific certifications) more than a checklist or tips post would on the same topic.
