A client called me two years ago convinced her WOSB certification was broken. "We've had it for 18 months," she said. "No federal revenue. Not one award. I'm starting to think certifications just don't work."

She wasn't wrong to be frustrated. But the certification wasn't broken. The foundation was missing.

WOSB, 8(a), SDVOSB, HUBZone — these are multipliers. They give you access to set-aside contract pools, sole-source authority, and agency small business goals. But they're multipliers of zero if you don't have a vehicle contracting officers can actually use to pay you. She didn't have a GSA Schedule. Buyers who wanted to work with her still had to run a full, separate acquisition process. Most didn't have the bandwidth. Most moved on.

Eleven months after we got her Schedule approved, her first federal award came in. Her WOSB certification was listed right there in the award summary. The certification had been working the whole time. It just needed something to amplify.

Certifications open doors. Your Schedule is the hallway that leads to them.
