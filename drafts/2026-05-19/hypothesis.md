This draft tests a **question hook** ("Before you apply for 8(a)... read this") on the **certifications** topic using a **bullets** format. The question hook was chosen because the ICP frequently makes the mistake of chasing certifications without understanding what each one actually unlocks — the hook creates a "stop and reconsider" moment that should drive curiosity-based opens. The bullets format structures each certification as a distinct insight, making it easy to scan and mentally match to the reader's own situation. The community question (which cert + which agencies) is designed to surface real-world context-sharing rather than generic agreement, which should drive **comments** over passive reactions. Reactions are expected to lift modestly from the broadly relatable topic; comments should outperform recent runs because the CTA asks for two specific, experience-based answers rather than one generic one.
