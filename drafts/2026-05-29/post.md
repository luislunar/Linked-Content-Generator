Most GSA Schedule holders have an active contract and no pipeline. I know that sounds blunt, but I see it constantly — approved vendors sitting nine, ten months in, waiting for the phone to ring.

The gap looks like this: no eBuy category alerts set up, no SAM.gov saved searches for sources sought notices, no list of recompetes tracked by agency. Just an approved contract number and an empty inbox. The contract was the goal. After approval, the work stopped.

Here's the uncomfortable truth: the Schedule doesn't source opportunities for you. It's an access tool, not a lead generator. You still have to hunt.

Start with three things — set eBuy category alerts in your SIN, build a SAM.gov sources sought watchlist in your top NAICS codes, and pull the forecast document for your two highest-priority agencies.
