Most small businesses treat their SAM.gov renewal like a checkbox to clear. It isn't.

Every time a client's 60-day window opens, we pull up their full profile — not just hit Renew. We audit every NAICS code first: outdated or missing codes make you invisible in agency searches, and we catch this more often than people expect. Then we cross-check the CAGE code, registered address, and banking info against current records. Then we go through the reps-and-certs section line by line. That last part gets skipped constantly — and stale answers there create compliance exposure that only surfaces at the worst moment.

The whole review takes less than an hour. But it only happens if someone is actually looking.
