Think pricing lower gives you a better shot at GSA awards?

Here's what it actually costs you:

Your GSA Schedule is a negotiated price ceiling. Discount commercially after award — you may owe that same discount to every federal customer. That's Basis of Award. Most applicants never hear about it until they're already filing a modification.

I talk to owners every week who locked in the wrong pricing structure on day one. By the time they realize it, renegotiation is on the table.

→ Audit your commercial pricing before you apply.
→ Know which customers trigger your Basis of Award.
→ Document every post-award discount you give.

The pricing mistake happens before you submit — not after.
