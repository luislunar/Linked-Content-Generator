Most small businesses I talk to want to skip straight to the IDIQ that looks biggest on paper.

Problem: the advanced vehicles have competition density that'll bury a new entrant. And most of them require past federal performance just to apply.

GSA MAS is agency-agnostic, has no prior federal performance requirement to get on, and is how most new federal contractors build the track record that makes the other vehicles viable.

The ceiling on MAS is high enough for most businesses to never need another vehicle.

Pick the vehicle that matches where your company is — not where you want it to be.
