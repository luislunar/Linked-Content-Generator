15 checkboxes.

→ SAM registration pending, not active
→ UEI doesn't match legal entity name exactly
→ Reps & certs not re-accepted in the last 365 days
→ Under 2 years of commercial revenue documented
→ References with dead phone numbers
→ MFC disclosure that doesn't match invoices
→ GSA pricing deeper than 8% below MFC (signals weakness)
→ Missing IFF in proposed prices
→ One TAA-non-compliant SKU in the catalog
→ Labor categories that don't map to commercial roles
→ "We will market our services" in Section M
