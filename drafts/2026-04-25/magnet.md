# The GSA Compliance Checklist: 15 Fixes Before You Submit

Run this list before you hit submit on eOffer. Each item below has rejected a real proposal in the last 24 months. 15 minutes now saves 6 months later.

## Registration & identity

- [ ] **1.** SAM.gov registration is **active**, not pending (check expiration date)
- [ ] **2.** UEI matches the legal entity name on every document
- [ ] **3.** CAGE code assigned and matches SAM
- [ ] **4.** Reps & certs re-accepted within the last 365 days
- [ ] **5.** Primary NAICS on SAM is appropriate for your proposed SIN(s)

## Past performance

- [ ] **6.** Minimum **2 years** of relevant commercial revenue documented
- [ ] **7.** 3–5 past-performance references with live contact info (phone + email verified)
- [ ] **8.** References have been notified they'll be contacted

## Pricing & commercial terms

- [ ] **9.** CSP-1 lists your Most Favored Customer (MFC) price accurately
- [ ] **10.** Proposed GSA pricing is 4–8% below MFC (not deeper, not shallower)
- [ ] **11.** Every SKU has commercial invoice backup (last 12 months)
- [ ] **12.** Pricing includes the 0.75% Industrial Funding Fee (IFF)

## Product & scope compliance

- [ ] **13.** Every SKU is TAA-compliant (US or designated country of origin)
- [ ] **14.** Labor categories map 1:1 to commercial role descriptions
- [ ] **15.** Section M (marketing) names specific target agencies + BPA strategy (not generic)

## What to do if any item is unchecked

Do NOT submit. Fix the item, then re-run the whole checklist. One unchecked item can unravel the other 14 — e.g., changing your MFC disclosure means re-validating every pricing line.
