This draft tests the myth-bust hook pattern in a carousel format after a 5-day absence from carousels. The myths topic was chosen because it hasn't appeared in the last 5 days and addresses a core ICP pain: small business owners paralyzed by misconceptions before they even attempt the GSA process. The three myths (past performance required, passive discoverability, IT-only coverage) were selected because they represent the highest-friction objections heard in real sales conversations — not abstract misconceptions. The carousel format is expected to lift saves and shares compared to single-card posts, since each slide functions as a portable reference. Comments should increase because the closing question ("Which myth almost stopped you?") invites specific, personal answers rather than vague affirmations — readers who believed one of these myths have a concrete story to share. Reactions are likely to follow from the credibility of the reality frames, which directly validate the reader's skepticism without making them feel foolish for believing the myth in the first place.
