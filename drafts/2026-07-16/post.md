Wrong reason to team up.

Most small businesses pursue teaming agreements because they think they need to fill a capability gap. Add a labor category they don't have. Cover a tech stack they can't staff.

That's fine. But it's not the reason teaming is actually powerful in GovCon.

The real value: it's a credibility transfer.

Past performance is the invisible wall every new entrant hits. You can't win a federal contract without it. You can't get it without winning. Classic catch-22.

Subcontracting on the right prime's contract breaks that loop. You build documented performance. Real CPARS ratings. Dollar values you can cite in your next proposal as your own.

That's not a capability story. That's a track record — built on someone else's contract, but legitimately yours.

Find the right prime partnership before you focus on your Schedule application. The relationship compounds faster than any paperwork.
