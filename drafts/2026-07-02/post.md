Nobody talks about federal Q4 the way they should.

July through September is when agencies push out a disproportionate share of their annual budget. Not because they planned it that way — because of use-it-or-lose-it appropriations. Contracting officers have 90 days to obligate the remaining year. The pressure is real, and the spend is concentrated.

The small businesses that prepped their GSA Schedule 6–12 months ago? They're getting calls right now.

The ones who kept saying "we'll start the application soon"? They're watching.

That's the real cost of delay in GovCon. It's not abstract. It's fiscal cycles. Miss this Q4, and your next meaningful window is October when the new fiscal year opens — but by then, you'd better already be in the system.
