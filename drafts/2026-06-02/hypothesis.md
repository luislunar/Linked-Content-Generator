This draft tests a question-led myth-bust hook on the teaming topic — an area where practitioners have strong personal opinions but where educational content is underrepresented in the GovCon feed. The hook ("You have a signed teaming agreement. Do you know what it actually protects you from?") challenges the reader's assumed knowledge directly, which creates tension before the reveal. The prose format (no bullets) was chosen because the hot_take type performs better as a flowing argument than a list — the point builds paragraph by paragraph toward the actionable ask. The community question specifically invites "hard-learned clause" stories, which are the kind of concrete, experience-based responses that drive high comment velocity. Expected to move comments more than reactions: the question is specific enough that govcon practitioners who have been burned on workshare splits will feel compelled to share their story. Reactions should still be solid because the myth-bust lands a truth most people quietly know but haven't said out loud.
